About Me

Incoming Computer Science freshman at UC Berkeley. I build AI-powered tools for real problems — smart glasses that help visually impaired users navigate independently, drone systems that bring healthcare to rural areas, and models that make sense of complex medical data. I came to this work personally: after experiencing vision challenges myself, assistive technology stopped feeling like a niche and started feeling like the whole point.
